## Step 1 — Connect your wallet

Go to [ordsbot.com](https://ordsbot.com) and click **Connect Wallet**.

OrdsBot uses **BIP-322 wallet authentication** — you prove ownership of your Bitcoin address by signing a short message. No seed phrase is ever shared.

<Note>
During the beta, access is whitelisted. If you see "Address not whitelisted", contact the team on Discord to request access.
</Note>

Supported wallets:
- **Xverse** (recommended)
- **Unisat**
- **Leather**

---

## Step 2 — Derive your bidding wallet

The bidding bot needs signing keys in memory to place bids on Satflow. Go to **Settings → Wallet** and enter your seed phrase.

<Warning>
Your seed phrase is entered in your browser only. All key derivation happens locally in your browser — only the private signing keys are sent to the bot's server memory for automated bid signing. Your seed phrase never leaves your device. Keys are held in server RAM only and are lost on restart — you will be prompted to re-derive automatically.
</Warning>

After deriving, you'll see:
- **Payment address** (bc1q...) — your native SegWit address
- **Ordinals address** (bc1p...) — your taproot address for inscriptions
- **Bidding wallet** — the Satflow multi-sig address where bid collateral is held

---

## Step 3 — Add your Satflow API key

In **Settings → API Keys**, paste your Satflow API key.

Don't have one? [Open a ticket on Satflow Discord](https://discord.gg/satflow) to request a key.

---

## Step 4 — Fund your bidding wallet

After deriving your wallet, your **Satflow bidding address** is shown in **Settings → Wallet**. This is a Satflow-managed multi-sig address — send Bitcoin here to fund your bids.

<Note>
You can also check your bidding address at any time in the Settings page after wallet setup.
</Note>

---

## Step 5 — Create your first task

Go to **Tasks → New Task** and fill in:

| Field | Example | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Collection slug | `bitcoin-puppets` | From the Satflow URL |
| Type | `ordinals` | Or `rune` for Runes |
| Bid mode | `% of Floor` | Or Fixed sats |
| Range | 75% – 85% | Bids at 75–85% of floor |
| Bid count | `5` | Number of concurrent bids |
| Hardcap | `0.001 BTC` | Max price per bid |

Hit **Save**, then click **Start**.

---

## Step 6 — Watch it run

The bot cycle runs every **30 seconds** by default. In the task detail view you can see:
- Current floor price
- Your active bids and prices
- Last cycle status
- Fill history

Telegram notifications are coming soon. Check the dashboard for fill history in the meantime.
</Tip>
