## Beta Program

OrdsBot is currently in **closed beta**. Beta users get full access to the platform at no cost for 90 days.

To join the beta, contact the team on Discord with your Bitcoin address and you'll be added to the whitelist.

| Plan | Price | Tasks | Bids per task | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Beta** | Free | 100 | 10 | 90 days |
| **Basic** | 50,000 sats/mo | 3 | 5 | 30 days |
| **Pro** | 150,000 sats/mo | 10 | 20 | 30 days |
| **Whale** | 500,000 sats/mo | 25 | 50 | 30 days |

<Note>
Prices are in sats (satoshis). 1 BTC = 100,000,000 sats.
</Note>

## Platform Limits

In addition to per-plan limits, the following platform-wide limits apply:

| Limit | Default |
|---|---|
| Max total exposure | 5,000,000 sats |
| Satflow API rate | 5 req/s, 10 burst |
| Floor cache TTL | 60 seconds |
| Challenge expiry | 5 minutes |

### Total Exposure

The **total exposure** is the sum of all active bid values across all your tasks. Once the limit is reached, the bot will not place new bids until existing ones are cancelled or filled.

You can check your current exposure in **Settings → Usage**.

## Payments

Paid plans are processed via **BTCPay Server** — fully on-chain, no custodian. You'll receive a Bitcoin invoice when purchasing a plan. Payment confirmation triggers immediate plan activation.
