## Beta Program

OrdsBot is currently in **closed beta**. Beta users get full access to the platform at no cost.

To join the beta, contact the team on [X](https://x.com/SatflowBot) / [Discord](https://discord.gg/auxdWwcw) with your Bitcoin address and you'll be added to the whitelist.

## Payments

Paid plans are processed via **BTCPay Server** - fully on-chain, no custodian. You'll receive a Bitcoin invoice when purchasing a plan. Payment confirmation triggers immediate plan activation.
